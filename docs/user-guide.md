## Understanding the Dashboard

### Account Overview
- **Balance**: Your current account balance
- **Equity**: Balance + unrealized P&L
- **Total P&L**: Overall profit/loss

### Safety Indicator

The safety indicator uses advanced market analysis to determine if conditions are favorable for trading:

- **ðŸŸ¢ GREEN (Safe)**
  - Low volatility
  - Normal spreads
  - No major news events
  - Best time to trade

- **ðŸŸ  ORANGE (Caution)**
  - Moderate volatility
  - Slightly wider spreads
  - Trade with increased monitoring

- **ðŸ”´ RED (Unsafe)**
  - High volatility
  - Wide spreads
  - Major news events
  - EA automatically disabled for your protection

### EA Control

**Start EA**
- Only available when indicator is GREEN or ORANGE
- Begins automated trading
- Copies trades from master account

**Stop EA**
- Immediately stops new trades
- Existing positions remain open
- Can be stopped at any time

### Trade History

View all your trading activity:
- **Open Trades**: Currently active positions
- **Closed Trades**: Completed trades with results

### P&L Details

- **Gross Profit**: Total profit from all trades
- **Commission**: Broker commissions paid
- **Swap**: Overnight holding costs
- **Net P&L**: Final profit after all costs

## Best Practices

### Risk Management

1. **Start Small**
   - Begin with minimum account size ($500)
   - Gradually increase as you gain confidence

2. **Monitor Regularly**
   - Check dashboard daily
   - Review open positions
   - Monitor P&L trends

3. **Respect Safety Indicator**
   - Don't force trading during RED periods
   - Exercise caution during ORANGE periods
   - Best results during GREEN periods

4. **Set Expectations**
   - This is an algorithmic system
   - Not every trade will be profitable
   - Focus on long-term results

### When to Stop EA

- Large unrealized losses
- Extended RED indicator period
- Account drawdown exceeds comfort level
- Personal trading strategy changes

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the minimum account size?**
A: We recommend minimum $500 for proper risk management.

**Q: Can I withdraw my profits anytime?**
A: Yes, withdraw directly from your broker account.

**Q: What happens during RED indicator?**
A: EA is automatically disabled to protect your account during unsafe conditions.

**Q: Can I manually trade alongside the EA?**
A: Not recommended. Manual trades may interfere with EA strategy.

**Q: How often should I check my account?**
A: Check daily, but trust the automated system. Over-monitoring can lead to emotional decisions.

**Q: What if I want to stop using the service?**
A: Simply stop the EA from dashboard and withdraw your funds from broker.
